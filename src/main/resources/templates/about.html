Our story

For the thousands of rescue animal foster parents currently caring for a rescued pet there is no central online resource that assists the adoption transition process. After having gone through the experience personally, I identified this as a major gap in the animal adoption sector that we are now addressing with Fosterstory.com. Many rescue animal foster parents are required carry the full burden of care, and also expected to take on the adoption marketing responsibilities. The purpose of Fosterstory.com is to ultimately connect prospective “Forever Families” to the inspiring and heart warming stories of rescuing and caring for these animals. Fostrestory will leverage existing social media infrastructure to allow for the simple creation, presentation, and marketing of these amazing rescue stories.
